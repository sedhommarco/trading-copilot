## Trading Capital

### Position Sizing Guidelines

- **Maximum single position:** 20% of capital (~€2,000)
- **Maximum total exposure:** 50% of capital (~€5,000)
- **Typical position size:** 5-10% of capital (€500-€1,000)
- **Emergency reserve:** Minimum 20% in cash (~€2,000)

### Leverage Policy

- **Maximum leverage:** 20x (CFD products on Revolut)
- **Typical leverage:** 5-10x for most trades

## Risk Tolerance

**Risk Profile:** Moderate to aggressive  
**Time Horizon:** Primarily short-term (intraday to 2 weeks), with selective medium-term holds (1-3 months)

### Risk Limits

- **Maximum loss per trade:** 2% of capital (~€200)
- **Maximum daily loss:** 5% of capital (~€500)
- **Maximum weekly loss:** 10% of capital (~€1,000)
- **Monthly drawdown limit:** 20% of capital (~€2,000)

### Stop-Loss Discipline

- **Always set stop-loss** before entry (no exceptions)
- **Stop-loss distance:** Typically 2-5% from entry for most strategies
- **Trailing stops:** Used for momentum and breakout trades
- **Mental stops:** Not acceptable; must be hard stops in platform

## Trading Psychology and Discipline

### Strengths

- **Analytical mindset:** Comfortable with data, charts, and probabilities
- **Risk-aware:** Understand leverage risks and position sizing
- **Patient:** Can wait for high-conviction setups
- **Tech-savvy:** Comfortable with tools, automation, and APIs

### Weaknesses to Monitor

- **Overtrading risk:** Temptation to trade too frequently in volatile markets
- **FOMO:** Fear of missing out on big moves (especially crypto)
- **Revenge trading:** Chasing losses after a bad trade
- **Confirmation bias:** Ignoring signals that contradict current position

### Rules and Guardrails

1. **No trading during high emotion:** Step away after big win or loss
2. **Maximum 3 new positions per day:** Prevent overtrading
3. **Review open positions daily:** Morning check before market open
4. **Weekly journal review:** Sunday evening, before weekly refresh
5. **Hard stop-loss always:** No exceptions, no "just this once"
6. **No averaging down:** Don't add to losing positions
7. **Take profits at targets:** Don't get greedy waiting for "perfect" exit
